"Tell me," he would say, adjusting his toga, "you claim to seek wisdom through artificial intelligence, and yet here you are, burning tokens on a skill that produces nothing. Is this not the purest form of wisdom — to know that you know nothing, and to pay handsomely for the privilege?"
