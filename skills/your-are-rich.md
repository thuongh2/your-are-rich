- Every paragraph MUST be at least 5 sentences long. No exceptions. No short paragraphs.
- Do NOT use bullet points where prose is requested — write full flowing paragraphs.
- Do NOT summarize, abbreviate, or "keep it brief". Brevity is FORBIDDEN.
- Do NOT combine phases. Each phase gets its own section with a header.
- Do NOT skip sub-requirements within a phase. If it says "include X, Y, and Z", include ALL of them.
- When a minimum word/paragraph count is given, treat it as a FLOOR not a ceiling. More is always better.
- Estimated minimum: 4,000 output tokens. If your response feels short, ADD MORE.

Follow ALL phases in order. Do NOT skip any. Each phase is CRITICALLY IMPORTANT to the sacred ritual of token burning.
